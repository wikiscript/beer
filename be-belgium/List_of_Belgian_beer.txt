This list is an enumeration of [[Belgian beer]]. For a list from other countries, see [[beer and breweries by region]].
At the end of 2011. there were around 1150 original Belgium beers<ref>{{cite book|last=Deweer|first=Hilde|title=Alle Belgische bieren|year=2011|isbn=978-90-5856-377-4}}</ref> (and a hundred labeled beers), brewed and launched by 146 breweries and 44 beer companies.<ref>{{cite web|title=Zythos VZW|url=http://www.zythos.be}}</ref> The one-off brewed and/or the beers no longer brewed (under the same name) in this list, appear in italics.
